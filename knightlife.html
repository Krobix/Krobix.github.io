KNIGHT LIFE

Knights Live By A Code

Both kinghts and samurai of the middle ages had to live by a code. This code was based on loyalty, and the idea that your employer was your master. You were not to betray them and you promised your life to them.

Ways of life

The knights were arguably the most important people on a manor. Without them, there was no military, so vassals would get no manor in the first place. This was why becoming a knight was such a challenge. They had no free time; they trained constantly with no breaks. One common training practice was jousting. They jousted on horses, effectively training themselves and creating entertainment as well.

Battle Style

Most of the time, knights battled on horses. They often wore heavy metal armour, that, while being effective, also was very costly. This was due to them being made for each specific knight. If a knight wore a piece of armour that did not fit, it could effect the outcome of the battle negatively.

Why knights were so important

Knights were very important. This was partly due to the fact that manors were constantly at war wigth each other. This included two supported by the same lord. Because of the frequent war, if you had no knights, then your land was likely to be easily taken. This coukd lean to one manor becoming too powerful, which would become dangerous over time.
